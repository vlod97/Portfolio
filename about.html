ABOUT

Originally from Ukraine I had moved to the UK where I studied Product Design as part of my GCSE's and A-Level's. Now I am on my second year studying Product Desing at the University of Sussex and am looking for a placement that will make use of my technical skills in CAD.

I believe that computer aided design will have an increasingly rising presence in commercial manufacture and will be a valuable skill in many industries. I am very passionate about the possibilities CAD provides and it is the one thing I can spend countless uninterrupted hours working with which is why I want to make it into part of my career so that I can make money off of what I genuinly enjoy doing.

I am looking for any placement that allows me to continue working with CAD and provides experience of working with real commercial products and clients.
